Australian technologist and developer with a background in information security currently working on various projects. Current work and projects:

- [OpenNEM](https://opennem.org.au) ([GitHub](https://github.com/opennem)) - Open Source platform for Australian energy data run from the Climate College at the University of Melbourne.
- [Nineprimes](https://nineprimes.com) - Data Science and engineering advisory and development shop

Previously:

- [Infotorch](https://infotorch.org/social) ([GitHub](https://github.com/infotorch)) - Data Journalism project
- [BeatCovid](https://beatcov.org) ([GitHub](https://github.com/beatcovid)) - Open Source platform for running patient surveys during the onset of the COVID19 pandemic. Run from the University of Swinburne.
- [Techcrunch](http://www.techcrunch.com)
- [CrunchBase](http://www.crunchbase.com) and a number of other startups since 2000.
- [CrunchFund](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/crunchfund) in San Francisco where he sourced investments in [DigitalOcean](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/digitalocean), [GitLab](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/gitlab-com), [Streamable](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/streamable), [ReadMe.io](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/readme-io), [Pachyderm](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/pachyderm#/entity) and [prediction.io](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/predictionio#/entity).

University of Wollongong drop-out. Blogging since November ’99. Writer [at Techcrunch from 2007](http://techcrunch.com/author/nik-cubrilovic/). Named in Australia’s The Bulletin magazine [in 2007 as one of Australia’s ‘Smart 100’](http://wayback.archive.org/web/*/http://bulletin.ninemsn.com.au/article.aspx?id=266916).

Worked in the security field since the 90s publishing dozens of advisories around web application security, IIS, Windows NT, Linux etc. (under various aliases and [in my own name](http://0day.today/author/21985)). Today I am an advocate for user privacy, security, civil and online rights.
