# About

Australian engineer building AI products and data systems, based in Sydney, working globally. Currently shipping [SquirrelScan](https://squirrelscan.com) and a portfolio of  tools through [Nineprimes](https://nineprimes.com), my consultancy. I write here about agents, evals, fine-tuning, and the practical end of building with LLMs.

## Currently building

Shipping projects on [GitHub](https://github.com/nc9) including:

- **[SquirrelScan](https://squirrelscan.com)** ([GitHub](https://github.com/squirrelscan/squirrelscan)) — CLI and cloud platform for website security, performance and content auditing. 50k+ installs. 
- **[Open Electricity](https://openelectricity.org.au)** ([GitHub](https://github.com/opennem)) — open data platform for the Australian National Electricity Market.
- **[Nineprimes](https://nineprimes.com)** — data and ML consultancy. Engagements span energy data, agent infrastructure, and applied LLM work.

Other projects on [GitHub](https://github.com/nc9).

## Background

Online and shipping things since the late '90s. Wrote for [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/author/nik-cubrilovic/) from 2007 covering startups, security, and emerging tech, and helped source early investments at [CrunchFund](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/crunchfund) — including [DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com), [GitLab](https://gitlab.com), [Pachyderm](https://www.pachyderm.com), [ReadMe](https://readme.com), and [Streamable](https://streamable.com).

Before that, security research. Most-cited work: the 2011 [Facebook logged-out tracking exposé](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/technology-15091674) (covered by the NYT and BBC), the 2014 [MyGov vulnerabilities disclosure](https://www.smh.com.au/it-pro/security-it/revealed-serious-flaws-in-mygov-site-exposed-millions-of-australians-private-information-20140515-zrczw.html), the [iCloud and 2014 celebrity photo hack analysis](https://www.smh.com.au/digital-life/consumer-security/icloud-celebrity-photo-hack-texts-address-books-and-more-also-accessible-20140903-10bov5.html), and a piece on [using information leakage to trade stocks](https://www.itnews.com.au/news/how-an-aussie-hacker-used-information-leakage-to-trade-stocks-420171). Web app security advisories under various aliases since '96.

Also along the way: [BeatCovid](https://beatcov.org) ([GitHub](https://github.com/beatcovid)), an open-source COVID symptom-survey platform run with the University of Swinburne, and [Infotorch](https://infotorch.org/social), a data journalism project.

University of Wollongong drop-out. Blogging since November '99.
